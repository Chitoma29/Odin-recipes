Avocado and Tuna Tapas

Description

Living in Spain I have come across a literal plethora of tapas. This tuna stuffed avocado is a light, healthy dish that goes best with crisp white wines and crunchy bread. This recipe is great for experimenting with a variety of different vegetables, spices, and kinds of vinegar.

ingredients

  • 1 can solid white tuna packed in water, drained
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  • 3 green onions, thinly sliced, plus additional for garnish
  • ½ red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 dash balsamic vinegar
  • black pepper to taste
  • 1 pinch garlic salt, or to taste
  • 2 ripe avocados, halved and pitted

steps

  1. In a bowl, combine the drained canned tuna, chopped red onion, chopped cilantro or parsley, and any optional ingredients like diced tomatoes, diced bell peppers, or chopped jalapeños
  2. Spoon the prepared tuna mixture into the hollows of the avocado halves. Press down gently to pack the filling.
  3. Spoon the prepared tuna mixture into the hollows of the avocado halves. Press down gently to pack the filling
  4. Arrange the filled avocado halves on a serving platter
